Avenida da Liberdade

Lisbon is quite big but not all places are interesting. I have picked that are close to the city centre or accessible from the centre such as Marques de Pombal, Avenida de Liberdade, Praca da figueire, castelo de sao Jorge, Praca do comercio and river Tejo and so on.

Glória - Restauradores

Rossio is also one of the main attraction in the city center where we also have a train station which makes it easy to assess and reachable. In front of the train station we have Praça Dom Pedro IV and Fonte Norte do Rossio which are very photogenic and scenic in a good sunny day.
