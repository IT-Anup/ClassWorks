Praça dos Restauradores

Lisbon is quite big but not all places are interesting. I have picked that are close to the city centre or accessible from the centre such as Marques de Pombal, Avenida de Liberdade, Praca da figueire, castelo de sao Jorge, Praca do comercio and river Tejo and so on.

Avenida da Liberdade

Rossio is also one of the main attraction in the city center where we also have a train station which makes it easy to assess and reachable. In front of the train station we have Praça Dom Pedro IV and Fonte Norte do Rossio which are very photogenic and scenic in a good sunny day.

Glória - Restauradores

Ascensor da Glória also know as the tram that only goes up and down in Praca dos Restauradores is also a very scenic and photegenic place. The tram only goes up and down and has only 1 set of tracks for 2 trams. The tram also has this intresting shape and size because of its position going up hill.

Rossio

Marques de Pombal, Avenida de Liberdade are right in the centre and are easily accessible. You can catch any metro line and get there via the final blue or yellow line. From the airport you can catch the red line metro or the airport bus. Via the red line you must change the metro in station sao Sebastiao and get the blue line to get off at Restaurateurs.
